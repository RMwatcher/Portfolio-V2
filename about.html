About Me

My name is Richard Mora. I enjoy working with my hands weather they be physical like a bookshelf or digital with code.

I starting my programming journey since 2018 when someone handed me a reference book teaching HTML and CSS where I learn how to build my first static website. After spending a few months, I discovered that I love it so much that I started wonder where else I could learn more about making websites and that's where I discovered a program at my local college so I enrolled. Fast forward 5 years I graduated an Associate of Applied Technology Web Development and Bachelor of Arts in Digital Technology & Culture.

Since then, I've been developing my skills as a freelance web developer where I designed and developed websites across volunteer, freelance and professional projects. During this time, I have developed my skills in web design (Adobe PhotoShop, Adobe XD, Figma) and development (HTML, CSS, JavaScript, jQuery, WordPress, Wix, Bootstrap, Responsive Layout design, Node.js, Rest API) while working with new technologies to expand my skills set.

It was during these times where I rediscovered my interests in story telling weather it's through various media or code. My other interests include going on hikes, cooking and/or baking, playing video games, watching anime and attending conventions through the year. I look forward to speaking with you about your project.
